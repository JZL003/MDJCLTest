  Photo of the Month Rules

  How to submit photos:

  1. Email the historian (mzim1727@gmail.com) with Photo of the Month Submission as the subject of the email
  2. Include in the email
    1. Your name
    2. Your school
    3. Your grade
    4. Caption. These can be long or short (please not over one page though). Every caption should include:
      1. Name of student(s) who are subject of the photo (optional)
      2. Event that the photograph was taken at
      3. Description of what is happening in the photo
    5. Attached photo
  3. Send and wait in anticipation for the winner to be announced

  FAQ:


        Q: How many photos can I submit every month?
        A: Every individual can only submit once per month, but there is no limit to the amount of submissions from a school
        Q: What will the photos be judged on?
        A: The photos will be judged on their quality along with how well the photo showcases JCL activates and spirit (e.g. don't submit pictures that only consist of a person's face)
        Q: Where and when will the winner be announced?
        A: The winner will be emailed and their photo will be posted on the MDJCL Instagram and Facebook as soon as the winner is announced, and showcased in that month’s edition of The Torch. The winner will be announced a few days after the end of the month.
        Q: What are the prizes?
        A: The prize is having their photo showcased on Instagram, Facebook, The Torch, as well as earning a place in the state scrapbook.
        Q: Will I become famous by winning this contest?
        A: I guess you can only find out if you win.
